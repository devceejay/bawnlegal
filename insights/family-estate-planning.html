Family Estate Planning: Why It's Better to Start Early

Published July 2025 • By Bawn Legal Group

Estate planning isn't only for the wealthy, it's a practical step for anyone who wants peace of mind and to protect loved ones from future uncertainty.

Common misconceptions

Many people think estate planning is something to do “later.” In reality, waiting too long can complicate matters, lead to disputes, or even increase tax burdens.

Benefits of planning early

  • Ensure your wishes are documented clearly
  • Reduce potential conflicts among family members
  • Plan tax-efficiently to protect your assets
  • Account for changing life circumstances over time

Where to begin

Start by discussing your goals with your family and then meet with an estate planning lawyer. Together, you can create a will, consider trusts, and put other protections in place.
