## Features:

- Multi-page structure: Home, About, Practice Areas, Legal Insights (blog), Contact
- Practice areas displayed as cards to highlight services clearly
- Case results cards to build trust and credibility
- “Legal Insights” blog section to share updates and advice
- Team profiles to introduce lawyers and staff
- FAQs accordion for common client questions
- Testimonials section to add social proof
- Contact form to encourage client inquiries
- Fully responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile

## Technologies Used:

**HTML5** - Semantic layout and structure

**CSS3** - Custom styling with flexbox and grid, responsive design

**JavaScript** - Interactivity, dynamic content, and logic
