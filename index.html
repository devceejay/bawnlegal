Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about working with our firm and what to expect.

How do I schedule a consultation? +

You can fill out the contact form on our website, email us directly at contact@bawnlegal.com , or call our office. We'll get back to you to confirm a convenient time.

What should I bring to my first meeting? +

Any documents related to your matter (contracts, letters, court papers, etc.) are helpful. But if you're not sure, don't worry we'll guide you through what we need.

How long will my case take? +

It depends on the complexity of your matter, whether it's transactional or a dispute, and other external factors. We'll always keep you updated on expected timelines as your case progresses.

Do you charge for the initial consultation? +

For most matters, we offer a brief introductory call at no cost to understand your needs. If a more detailed review or advice is needed, we'll let you know any fees upfront.

How do you bill for your services? +

We typically bill hourly or offer fixed-fee arrangements for certain services, depending on the matter. Before we start work, we'll always explain your options clearly so there are no surprises.

How will I stay updated on my case? +

Communication is important to us. We keep clients updated by email or phone as your matter progresses, and you can always reach out if you have questions.
